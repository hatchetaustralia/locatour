**Locatour is a game you play by going outside.**

Instead of grinding levels on a couch, you level up by physically visiting real,
public places in the world (parks, lookouts, reserves, beaches, trails,
monuments, swimming spots) and **checking in** when you get there. Every
check-in earns you experience, and experience unlocks more of the map.

Think of it as a treasure hunt across your own neighbourhood, city, and state,
with the best treasures saved for the people who explore the most.

## The core loop

1. **Find a place.** Open the map and browse the locations near you.
2. **Go there.** Locatour is played with your feet: you have to actually be
   at a place to check in.
3. **Check in with a photo.** When you arrive, your phone confirms you're
   there and you snap a photo to mark the moment.
4. **Earn experience and level up.** Each check-in rewards you, and as you
   level up, new and more remote places open up.
5. **Repeat, and explore further.** The more you play, the more of the world
   you can reach.

## Who it's for

Locatour is for anyone who wants a reason to get out the door: solo explorers,
families looking for a weekend mission, locals who want to rediscover their own
backyard, and travellers who want to find the spots that aren't in every
guidebook.

## What makes it different

- **Real places only.** Every location is a genuine public, non-commercial
  place, owned by everyone and free to visit.
- **You earn the map.** The most special places are *hidden* until you've put
  in the exploring to unlock them. (More on that in
  [Hidden locations](/play/hidden-locations/).)
- **It's about memories, not points.** The points are just the scoreboard. The
  real reward is the experience of being somewhere you'd never have gone
  otherwise. That's the whole idea. See
  [Creating Memorable Experiences](/start/memorable-experiences/).
