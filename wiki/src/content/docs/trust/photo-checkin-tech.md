This page explains, in plain language, how Locatour confirms a check-in. No
maths, no secrets you don't need — just an honest picture of what happens when
you tap **check in**.

## What your phone checks

When you check in, two things come together:

1. **Your location.** Locatour asks your phone for its current GPS position and
   checks that you're at the place — not nearby-ish, but actually there. Your
   phone also tells us how confident it is in that position, and we take that
   into account so a slightly fuzzy reading on a cloudy day doesn't punish an
   honest visit.
2. **A live photo.** You take the photo right then, inside the app. It isn't
   pulled from your camera roll — it's shot on the spot, as part of being
   present.

If both line up — you're at the place, and you've taken a fresh photo there —
your check-in succeeds.

## Why we do it on the spot

Locatour only works if check-ins reflect real visits. Doing the location and
photo check **live, at the place**, is what keeps the game fair for everyone who
plays it honestly. It means your levels, streaks, and achievements actually
stand for something.

## Built to be fair, honestly explained

We're upfront about the limits. No phone-based check can *prove* presence with
total certainty — GPS can wander, and a determined person can try to trick any
app. So Locatour is designed in layers: friendly checks on your phone give you
instant feedback and stop casual mistakes, and additional checks happen behind
the scenes to keep things fair at scale.

We deliberately don't publish the exact thresholds and rules. That's not to be
secretive with honest players — it's so the fairness checks keep working. **If
you actually went to the place, you have nothing to think about.**

## When something goes wrong

Most failed check-ins are simple and fixable:

- **Not close enough?** Move nearer to the exact spot and retry.
- **Weak GPS?** Step into the open, away from tall buildings or thick tree
  cover, and try again — your phone needs a clear view of the sky.
- **Location off?** Enable location services and Locatour's location
  permission.
- **Offline?** Locatour can save the check-in and finish verifying it when
  you're back online.
