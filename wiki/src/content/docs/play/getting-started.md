Welcome! Here's everything you need to make your first check-in and start
levelling up.

## 1. Set up your profile

When you first open Locatour, you'll create an account and set up a quick
profile — a name, an avatar, and your **interests** (hiking, camping, fishing,
photography, and more). Your interests help the app surface the kinds of places
you'll enjoy most.

## 2. Find a place near you

Open the map. You'll see locations around you, each marked with a pin. Tap one
to see its details — what it is, what you'll find there, and what you'll earn
for visiting.

At the start, you'll see the everyday, easy-to-reach places near you. The more
remote and hidden spots stay locked until you've levelled up — that's part of
the fun. See [Finding & unlocking locations](/play/locations/).

## 3. Go there

This is the part that matters: **you have to actually be at the place.**
Locatour is played outdoors, on foot. Pick somewhere you can get to, and head
out.

## 4. Check in with a photo

When you arrive, open the location and tap **check in**. Your phone confirms
you're really there, and you'll take a quick photo to mark the moment. That's
it — you've checked in.

Curious how the photo and location check works (and why)? See
[Photo check-ins](/play/photo-checkins/).

## 5. Earn experience and level up

Every check-in rewards you with experience. As your experience grows, you climb
levels — and each milestone unlocks new places to discover. Keep a daily habit
going and you'll build a **streak**, too.

## Tips for your first week

- **Start close.** Knock out a few easy, nearby places to get a feel for the
  loop.
- **Turn on location and camera permissions.** Locatour needs both to verify a
  check-in — here's [exactly what we do with them](/trust/data-and-security/).
- **Follow your interests.** Places that match your interests are a great place
  to begin.
- **Come back tomorrow.** Daily check-ins build streaks and add up fast.
