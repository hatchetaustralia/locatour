<Steps>

1. **Sign in and set up your profile.**

   When you first open Locatour, sign in with Google to create your account, then
   set up a quick profile — a name, an avatar, and your **interests** (hiking,
   camping, fishing, photography, and more). Your interests help the app surface
   the kinds of places you'll enjoy most.

2. **Find a place near you.**

   Open the map. You'll see locations around you, each marked with a pin. Tap
   one to see its details — what it is, what you'll find there, and what you'll
   earn for visiting. At the start you'll see the everyday, easy-to-reach
   places; the more remote and hidden spots stay locked until you've levelled
   up. See [Finding & unlocking locations](/play/locations/).

3. **Go there.**

   This is the part that matters: **you have to actually be at the place.**
   Locatour is played outdoors, on foot. Pick somewhere you can get to, and
   head out.

4. **Check in with a photo.**

   When you arrive, open the location and tap **check in**. Your phone confirms
   you're really there, and you'll take a quick photo to mark the moment.
   That's it — you've checked in. Curious how it works (and why)? See [Photo
   check-ins](/play/photo-checkins/).

5. **Earn experience and level up.**

   Every check-in rewards you with experience. As your experience grows, you
   climb levels — and each milestone unlocks new places to discover. Keep a
   daily habit going and you'll build a **streak**, too.

</Steps>

<Aside type="tip" title="Tips for your first week">
- **Start close.** Knock out a few easy, nearby places to get a feel for the loop.
- **Turn on location and camera permissions.** Locatour needs both to verify a
  check-in — here's [exactly what we do with them](/trust/data-and-security/).
- **Follow your interests.** Places that match your interests are a great place to begin.
- **Come back tomorrow.** Daily check-ins build streaks and add up fast.
</Aside>
