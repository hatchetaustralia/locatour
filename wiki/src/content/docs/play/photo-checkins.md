A **check-in** is how you prove you made it. It's the single action at the
centre of the whole game: you're at a place, you check in, you earn experience.

## How a check-in works

1. **You arrive at the place.** Check-ins are proximity-based — you have to
   actually be there.
2. **Your phone confirms you're there.** Locatour uses your device's location
   to check that you're at the spot, not somewhere else.
3. **You take a photo.** You snap a quick photo, live, in the app — a little
   memento of the moment and your visit.
4. **You're checked in.** Experience is awarded, your streak ticks over, and
   the moment is saved to your history.

## Why a photo?

Three reasons, in order of importance:

- **It's the memory.** The photo is the souvenir — the thing you'll scroll back
  through later. Locatour is about [creating memorable
  experiences](/start/memorable-experiences/), and the photo is how each one
  gets saved.
- **It marks that you were present.** Taking a photo on the spot is part of
  being *there*, not just passing through.
- **It keeps the game fair.** Real check-ins from real visits are what make
  levels and achievements mean something.

## Keeping it fair

Locatour is a game of genuine visits, so we take fairness seriously. The app is
designed to recognise a real, in-person check-in and to gently discourage ways
of faking one. We keep the exact details of those checks under the hood — partly
so they keep working, and partly because honest players never need to think
about them. If you actually went, you're fine.

If a check-in doesn't go through, it's almost always something simple — see the
troubleshooting tips below.

## If a check-in doesn't work

- **"You're not close enough."** Get a bit nearer to the exact spot and try
  again.
- **"We can't pin down your location."** Step into the open, away from tall
  buildings or dense tree cover, and retry — your phone needs a clear view of
  the sky for an accurate fix.
- **Location is turned off.** Make sure location services and Locatour's
  location permission are enabled.
- **You're offline.** No signal? No problem — Locatour can save your check-in
  and finish it when you're back online.
